About me

Hello! I'm Parth Prajapati, a 3D Artist and Multimedia Designer. My academic journey in Game and Level Design, combined with a Bachelor's in Computer Application in Design, has fueled my passion for creating immersive game environments and engaging digital content.

Throughout my career, I've gained diverse experiences as a 3D Modeler, VFX Intern, and Motion Graphic Video Advert Designer. These roles have honed my skills in Autodesk Maya, Unreal Engine 5, and Adobe Creative Suite, enabling me to merge technical precision with artistic creativity.

I'm particularly passionate about revolutionizing the gaming sector. My goal is to enhance game environments and player engagement, using my expertise in software like Substance Painter and ZBrush to bring dynamic designs to life.

As I look forward to my next challenge, I am eager to join a dynamic team where I can contribute my technical skills and creative insights, creating games and digital experiences that captivate audiences globally.
